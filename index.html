Privacy Policy

Year In Progress Chrome Extension

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your information never leaves your device.

What Data We Collect

Year In Progress collects minimal information to function:

  • Your First Name: Stored locally in your browser's localStorage to personalize your greeting

That's it. We do not collect:

  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics
  • Any other personal information

How We Use Your Data

Your first name is used solely to display a personalized greeting (e.g., "Good morning, [Your Name]") when you open a new tab.

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Can be changed or deleted at any time

Third-Party Services

Year In Progress loads the Inter font family from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to ensure consistent typography. This is the only external resource used.

Google Fonts may collect basic usage data as outlined in their privacy policy. We do not control or access this data.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using your browser's localStorage API. This means:

  • Data persists only on your device
  • We have no access to your data
  • Clearing browser data will remove your stored name

Cookies

Year In Progress does not use cookies.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools. We have no visibility into how you use the extension.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access from our end.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Your name is visible in the extension interface
  • Modify: Click "Change name" to update your information
  • Delete: Uninstall the extension or clear browser data

Children's Privacy

Year In Progress does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

Consent

By using Year In Progress, you consent to this privacy policy.
